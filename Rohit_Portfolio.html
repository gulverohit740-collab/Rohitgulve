Understanding Type 2 Diabetes – Causes, Symptoms & Prevention

Intro: Type 2 diabetes is a chronic condition in which the body becomes resistant to insulin or does not produce enough insulin to maintain normal blood sugar levels. Early lifestyle changes can prevent or delay the onset.

Key points: Causes — poor diet, sedentary lifestyle, genetics, obesity. Symptoms — frequent urination, excessive thirst, fatigue. Prevention — balanced diet, regular exercise, health check-ups.

Benefits of Vitamin D for Bone Health

Intro: Vitamin D plays a vital role in calcium absorption and bone health. Adequate Vitamin D supports immunity and prevents conditions like rickets and osteoporosis.

Sources: Sunlight, fatty fish, egg yolk, fortified milk; supplements when prescribed.
